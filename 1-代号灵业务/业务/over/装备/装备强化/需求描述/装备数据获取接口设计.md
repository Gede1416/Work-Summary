# 输入 
1. ClientEquipmentData
    - 表 hero_equipment
    - 网络消息 EquipmentData

# 输出
1. 等级 from: hero_equipment
2. 强化次数 from: EquipmentData
3. 名称 from: hero_equipment
4. 当前强化等级 from: EquipmentData
5. 预计强化等级 from: EquipmentData 后续计算
6. 当前属性 from: hero_equipment,EquipmentData 
7. 预计属性 from: hero_equipment,EquipmentData

# 输出计算公式
1. 属性计算
    - 强化后基础属性 = 装备基础属性 × (1 + 强化加成系数)
    - 装备基础属性:hero_equipment->basic_attribute_value
    - 强化加成系数:hero_equipment_enhance->basic_attribute_bonus
